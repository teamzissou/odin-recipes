Fried Rice

Description

This is a description of fried rice

You can put almost anything in your fried rice, but egg, peppers, scallions, and another protein of choice are classics. This is better with rice cooked the day before.

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Peppers
  • Egg (scrambled)
  • Scallions, chopped
  • Chicken

Steps

  1. Heat a wok with some neutral oil
  2. Add you chicken or other protein, cook until cooked through
  3. add peppers and cook
  4. add rice and cook until warmed
  5. make well in middle of wok and add egg, cook until scrambled
