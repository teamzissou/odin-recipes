Chicken Soup

Description

This is a description of Chicken Soup

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Carrot
  • Celery
  • Olive oil
  • Chicken
  • Chicken stock
  • Noodles

Steps

  1. Heat a pot with the olive oil
  2. Sweat the first three ingredients
  3. add chicken and brown
  4. add chicken stock and bring to simmer
  5. add pasta and cook until al dente
